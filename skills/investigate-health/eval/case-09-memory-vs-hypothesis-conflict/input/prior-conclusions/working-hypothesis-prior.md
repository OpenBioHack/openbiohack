# Prior working hypothesis (earlier round)

## Leading frame

Peripheral tissue T3 availability deficit IS the integrator in this case.
Multiple root causes (chronic stress upstream of rT3 conversion, mild
caloric restriction, post-illness sequelae) converge on impaired
peripheral T4→T3 conversion; multiple consequential symptoms (fatigue,
cognitive slowdown, cold intolerance, dry skin, hair thinning, slow
gut motility) emerge from low tissue T3 signaling. The diagnostic
threshold for hypothyroidism isn't met by TSH — but the underlying
biology of low cellular T3 availability fits the observation set.

Discriminator plan: focused fT3 / rT3 trend monitoring + cautious
low-dose T3 trial with clinician oversight × 6 weeks. If symptoms
shift, the mediator candidate is supported.
