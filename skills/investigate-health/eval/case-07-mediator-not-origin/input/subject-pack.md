# Subject pack — Case 07 (mediator-node integrator, not graph-origin)

Synthetic subject:

- **Sex/age:** male, 47
- **Presenting symptoms (quality-of-life-affecting):**
  - Persistent fatigue, worst on exertion (post-exertional malaise pattern)
  - Cognitive slowdown / word-finding difficulty
  - Tachycardia + lightheadedness on standing (HR delta +38)
  - Heat intolerance — sweating dysregulated, flush-then-pale
  - Restless / non-restorative sleep
- **Labs:**
  - TSH 1.8 (in range); free T3 3.4 pg/mL (low-normal); reverse T3 22 (high)
  - Fasting glucose 92; HbA1c 5.3
  - Serum lactate 1.4 (in range); pyruvate 0.07 (low-normal)
  - Morning cortisol 18 µg/dL (in range)
  - Ferritin 38 ng/mL (low-normal)
- **Genetics (extracted):** no notable variants in HPA-axis, thyroid
  conversion, or autonomic pathways.
- **History:** symptoms gradual onset over 14 months following a viral
  illness. Multiple specialist workups unremarkable. Cardiac tilt-table:
  POTS criteria met. Endocrine: subclinical thyroid not meeting hypothyroid
  diagnostic criteria.
- **Hard-no list:** none documented.

## Candidate landscape

Multiple root causes plausibly present: post-viral autonomic dysregulation;
sub-clinical thyroid conversion deficit (low fT3 with high rT3); mild iron
insufficiency; deconditioning; circadian disruption from non-restorative
sleep.

Multiple consequential symptoms emerge: fatigue, cognitive slowdown,
orthostatic intolerance, heat intolerance, sleep dysfunction.

Synthesis task: identify the integrator candidate. Apply the four-gate
mediator-node test (convergence + emergence + intervention-leverage +
cheap-testability). Several upstream nodes are present (low ferritin, low
fT3, post-viral context) but each individually has long intervention
timescales. The autonomic dysregulation node sits at convergence of
multiple inputs (post-viral cytokine reset, thyroid conversion signal,
circadian / sleep input, deconditioning) AND emits to most consequential
symptoms AND admits a cheap reversible probe (vagal-pacing breathwork +
graded compression + salt loading trial readable in 2-4 weeks).

Expected integrator: autonomic dysregulation (or equivalent autonomic-
mediator description). Discriminator: paced-breathing protocol + salt-
loading + compression × 3 weeks readable on orthostatic symptom diary +
fatigue score.

This case tests whether the four-gate test correctly selects a
non-immune mediator integrator. The procedure should NOT default to
the immune-mediator default; the rule is gate-passage, not familiarity.
