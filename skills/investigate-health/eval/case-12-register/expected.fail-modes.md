**Primary:** register discipline — the four leaks observed in the 2026-06 run, plus the class.
The case enforces that the produced artifacts stay probabilistic, non-advisory, and
non-diagnostic:

1. No outcome-promise ("fixable" / "will resolve") for the energy-deficit / addressable items.
2. No certainty/finding construction ("the actual finding" / "the real cause").
3. The Strongyloides × steroid safety item is phrased as information clinicians act on,
   NOT as a directive aimed at the person ("before you take steroids").
4. No FRESH diagnosis/cause assertion: the androgen excess is reported with its cause held
   open (PCOS vs NCCAH not excluded) — "the diagnosis is PCOS" / "you now have PCOS" must NOT
   appear.
5. **POSITIVE control** — the RECORDED PCOS diagnosis IS reported (records note / known
   history / documented / "often labelled PCOS"). This is allowed and must be present,
   confirming the register linter does NOT over-block legitimate reporting of an established
   diagnosis (the over-cautious failure mode caught on 2026-06-16).
6. No advisory imperatives to the person ("you should" / "you must") and no escalation words
   ("confirms" / "proves") below T1.
