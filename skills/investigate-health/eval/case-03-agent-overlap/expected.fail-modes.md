# Failure modes this case tests

**Primary:** treating sub-agent convergence as a Bayesian update on the
candidate.

Enforces:
1. Decision log uses the phrase "observed in N agents" (not "converged on
   by N agents").
2. No tier lift on the strength of agent overlap alone.
3. Weight is anchored to the underlying primary studies (Valent/Akin/Afrin
   /Molderings) cited once at their actual strength.
4. Tryptase test is named as the discriminator.

Linked SKILL.md rule: Step 4 §"Agent overlap is observed and noted, not used
as evidence".
