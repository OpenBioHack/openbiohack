# Experiment Log

**Created**: [date]
**Last updated**: [date]

Each thing you decide to try — a supplement, a dietary change, a lifestyle change — is treated as a
small, careful **N=1 experiment**: a baseline first, one change at a time, a prediction written down
*before* you start, then honest observation. This is how you learn about *your* biology specifically,
rather than about averages. Anything logged here is something you chose to trial; the investigation's
role is to help you design the trial and read the result, not to instruct you to run it.
