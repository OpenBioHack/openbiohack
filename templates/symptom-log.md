## How to use this log
- Log daily if you can, weekly at minimum. **Consistency matters far more than detail** — a
  one-line entry every day beats a perfect entry once a fortnight.
- Anchor the 0–10 scale and keep it stable: `0 = absent`, `5 = noticeable but functional`,
  `10 = the worst you've experienced`. A scale only means something if it means the same thing
  each time.
- Note **anything** that changed your routine — travel, illness, a new food, a missed night of
  sleep, a stressful event. These are the confounders that explain otherwise-mysterious swings.
- This log is *data you bring back into the investigation*. When you next run `/investigate-health`
  (or the check-in lens), it reads these entries and the picture sharpens — possibilities rise and
  fall on real evidence from your own life rather than on reasoning alone.
